Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
20 Mins 10 Mins 2 hrs 30 mins

Instructions

Step 1

Put water and powdered agar in a saucepan and boil it on fire
When it boils, heat it for about 2 minutes while mixing with low heat
Then stop the fire

Step 2

Add bean paste, sugar, salt and mix well
※ Please adjust the amount of sugar to see the taste

Step 3

Cook it on medium heat again and if it boils, you need to get down from the fire
and put the pot bottom to cold water
Mixes it until lightly thick

Step 4

Wet the inside of the container and gently pour the stuff that you made
※ The container that has no dents on the bottom is recommended

Step 5

Cool it in a refrigerator
Estimated time to complete is 2 to 3 hours
I have stayed more than half a day

Step 6

Take out from the container and cut it to your preference
